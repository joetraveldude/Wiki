##Blocked work item modifier tags:
- Cross Team Dependency - When a team cannot unblock an item without another team's help
- Product Dependency - When we are dependent upon a non-Blue Mountain Product Team to deliver a capability before we can move forward
- External Dependency - When we are dependent upon Defense Industrial Base (DIB) partner or other agency (DISA, etc.).
- Customer Dependency - When we are dependent upon the Blue Mountain customer
